Train from Canterbury. 27 September 2021.
Wolf Alice’s Don’t Delete the Kisses.
~/Centre~

Departing first my train. Then my tears.
And I was building a palimpsest of mistakes.
The palimpsest was my opacifying barrier
Without reflection.
Everybody tells me I was right;
The voices can still be heard.
Quiet, pathetic sobber.
Against the steady kick and a
Blur of nature.
By degrees 
In
Nothing I felt eveything.
But you were
Endangering a boy—and I you.
Never can I hate you;
I proclaim so to circles.
Tonight, sighs rattle my skull
So cold—so unexplained.
I hardly blame you for
Talking to me. But, O, the addiction!
Dealer unwittingly hooking with free sample of
Love. He doesn’t supply anymore.
END
Friday 06 May 2022
Jacob Fisher
