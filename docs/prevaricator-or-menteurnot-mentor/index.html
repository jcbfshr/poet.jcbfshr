Go back

Prevaricator, or menteur—not mentor!

I truly do detest you.
If you care, as your eyes show your soul,
You wouldn’t need to tango lexically.
Don’t let your eyes dodge me and your words touch me.
Don’t hug me to humour me.
Don’t palter with me to protract possibilites.
When angry, speak!
I don’t want to hate you.

I’m just doing the things as before
That deliver me to hurt again;
I wish life would stop driving me to poetry.
Sunday 15 May 2022
Jacob Fisher
