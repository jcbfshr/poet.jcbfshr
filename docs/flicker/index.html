Flicker

TVs flickering in the street, and
Strobing the docile faces, and
Pulsing the pleasures of another people.

Biharmonic stereo, play.
This mono thinking delay
Pushes us into dismay;
Ultimately the stereo stray.

Boring books burning bright:
Soot makes you stutter.
Darkness now hidden behind
The anthracite cloak.
Please don't let the salamander get me...

Listen, and listen well.
Seeming specious, how alien it seems!
Chanting chords: now a diminished art.
Say what you came to say, and go!
We're tired of the nude liberties;
We want the world to be blue.
~~~
D'you remember that? Blue.
Red blue red. Viscous and venous.
Sunday 06 June 2021
Jacob Fisher
