Hangman

Hang the man,
Guess the letter.
Fuck the man with
Your incessant stupidity.
~~~
Game of young;
Gamble of life.
Why do you summon
Me to your tall
Towers of bloodied
Execution?
~~~
Vowel, two consonants,
and another beam.
The noose tightens
As the pens strokes.

DEATH
IS
FREEDOM
Thursday 13 May 2021
Jacob Fisher
