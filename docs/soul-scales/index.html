Go back

soul scales

when
you were
here before,
things were different.

but now
that you're
gone I learnt
myself. although I lost
you,

my soul
and the importance of
you placed oppositely
on those old-fashioned scales:

gravity
doesn't decide for
me. so self-centred is
it, she, he, they (whoever
the fuck
YOU are)

that my soul's weight fluctuates to my heart's desire.
Saturday 25 December 2021
Jacob Fisher
